FAQ

Common questions about bathroom renovations in London.

Quick answers on cost, timing, scope and how we work with private clients, showrooms and interior designers.

How much does a bathroom renovation cost in London?+

Cost depends on the size of the room, scope of work, specification of materials and any structural changes. After a free consultation we provide a clear, itemised quote covering strip-out, plumbing, electrics, waterproofing, tiling and fitting — no hidden line items at the end.

How long does a full bathroom renovation take?+

A standard full bathroom renovation in London usually takes 7–12 working days from strip-out to final handover, depending on specification, tile choice, joinery and lead times of fittings.

Do you install wetrooms in London?+

Yes — wetroom installation is one of our core services. We handle drainage planning, full tanking and waterproofing, tiling and the final fit-out for residential wetrooms across London, including compact ensuite layouts.

Do you handle plumbing and electrics?+

Yes. Plumbing, pipework and electrical coordination are managed in-house as part of the wider installation process, using competent trades where regulated work is required.

Do you work with bathroom showrooms and interior designers?+

Yes — we work with London bathroom showrooms and interior designers as a trusted installation partner. We support partner projects with site visits, technical checks, installation and clean final handover on a partner-first basis. See the Partners section for details.

Do you provide waterproofing and tanking?+

Yes — full waterproofing and tanking of wet areas is included in every wetroom installation and offered as a standalone service for bathrooms where the original waterproofing has failed.

Can you visit the property before quoting?+

Yes. We carry out free, no-obligation site visits to check the space, services and access before producing a quote. For partner showroom projects we also do technical pre-installation checks.

Which areas of London do you cover?+

We work across London including Islington, Angel, Hackney, Shoreditch, Camden, Clerkenwell, and the wider North London and Central London areas. Other locations across Greater London are considered on a project basis.

Can you manage the full bathroom renovation from start to finish?+

Yes. Our service covers everything from initial consultation and strip-out through plumbing, electrics, waterproofing, tiling, fitting and final detailing — managed by one installation team and one point of contact.
