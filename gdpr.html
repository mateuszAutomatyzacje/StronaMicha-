UK GDPR

UK GDPR and data protection summary.

This page summarises how Build Masters Group Ltd approaches UK GDPR and data protection responsibilities in relation to enquiries, quotations, bathroom installation projects and client communication.

1. Controller 2. Lawful basis 3. Individual rights 4. Security and retention 5. Requests
01

Data controller

Build Masters Group Ltd acts as the controller for personal information processed through direct enquiries, quote requests, project communication and normal business administration.

  • The business decides why and how enquiry and client information is used.
  • Information is used in connection with bathroom renovations, wetrooms, tiling, waterproofing, plumbing and finishing services.
  • Questions about data protection can be sent to buildmastersgroup@gmail.com.
02

Lawful basis for processing

Depending on the situation, the business may rely on one or more lawful bases under UK GDPR.

  • Contract or pre-contract steps: responding to quote requests, arranging work and delivering agreed services.
  • Legitimate interests: managing enquiries, business records, client communication and service quality.
  • Legal obligation: keeping accounting, tax, insurance or compliance records where required.
  • Consent: where specific permission is requested, for example for public use of testimonials or project photos.
03

Your individual rights

Under UK data protection law, individuals may have rights relating to their personal information, depending on the circumstances.

  • Right of access — to ask for a copy of personal information held about you.
  • Right to rectification — to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion where information is no longer needed and no legal reason requires retention.
  • Right to restriction or objection — to limit or object to certain processing in relevant circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — where applicable to certain information provided electronically.
04

Security, retention and sharing

Build Masters Group Ltd aims to keep personal information practical, limited and relevant to the service being provided.

  • Personal information should be kept only for as long as needed for enquiries, projects, records and legal obligations.
  • Project details may be shared with trusted trades, suppliers or service providers only where needed for the work.
  • Business records may be kept for accounting, tax, insurance or legal reasons.
  • Client photos, references or testimonials should not be published publicly without appropriate permission.
05

How to make a request

To ask about your personal information or make a UK GDPR-related request, contact Build Masters Group Ltd with enough detail to identify the relevant enquiry or project.
