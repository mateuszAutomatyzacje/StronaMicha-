What cookies are

Cookies are small text files or browser storage items that may be placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies can include local storage, session storage, pixels or scripts used to support website functionality.

  • Some cookies are necessary for a website to work properly.
  • Some cookies help remember basic preferences or improve navigation.
  • Some optional tools can be used for analytics, tracking or marketing if they are added to a website.

How you can control cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete cookies, block cookies or receive alerts before cookies are stored.

  • Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge and other browsers provide cookie controls in their settings.
  • Deleting or blocking some technologies may affect website display or functionality.
  • If a cookie consent banner is added in the future, use its controls to manage optional cookies on this website.
