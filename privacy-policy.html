Privacy Policy

How we handle personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Build Masters Group Ltd may collect, how we use it, how long we may keep it and how you can contact us about your rights.

01

Information we may collect

When you contact Build Masters Group Ltd, request a quote, send photos or discuss a bathroom installation project, we may collect information that you provide directly.

  • Name and contact details, including phone number and email address.
  • Project address or approximate location where needed for quotation or planning.
  • Photos, videos, measurements, drawings or descriptions of the bathroom or property.
  • Messages, call notes, quote details and agreed project requirements.
  • Payment, invoice and business record information where a project goes ahead.
02

How we use personal information

We use personal information only for practical business purposes connected with our bathroom installation services.

  • To respond to enquiries and provide free quotes.
  • To assess the scope of a bathroom renovation, wetroom installation, tiling, plumbing or finishing project.
  • To arrange consultations, site visits, scheduling and project communication.
  • To prepare, send and manage quotations, invoices and business records.
  • To maintain safe, organised and professional service delivery.
03

Sharing, storage and retention

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only where necessary for the service, for business administration or where required by law.

  • Information may be shared with trusted trades, suppliers or service providers where needed for a project.
  • Accounting, insurance or legal records may be retained where required for business, tax or compliance reasons.
  • Project photos or references should not be published publicly without appropriate permission.
  • We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed for enquiries, projects, records and legal obligations.
04

Your privacy rights

Depending on the circumstances and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the use of your personal information.

  • You can ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • You can ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • You can ask us to delete information where it is no longer needed, subject to legal or business record requirements.
  • You can object to certain uses of your information where applicable.
