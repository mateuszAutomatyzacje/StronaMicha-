What "bathroom fitters" means at Build Masters

Not just fitting — the whole installation managed by one team.

Most clients in London search for "bathroom fitters" but actually need more than fitting: they need a team that can take the room from existing condition to finished bathroom without four different trades blaming each other when something goes wrong. That's how Build Masters Group Ltd works. The same team handles strip-out, plumbing, electrics, waterproofing, tiling and final fitting — so there's one point of contact, one quote and one accountability for the finish.

The fitting itself

Once the room is ready — services first-fixed, walls boarded, waterproofed and tiled — the fitting stage installs everything you can see and touch: the WC, basin, vanity, taps, bath or shower tray, glass screen, mirror, accessories and final brassware. Done well, this stage looks effortless. Done badly, it's where the project visibly fails — uneven silicone, misaligned screens, dripping taps, accessories drilled into the wrong place.

What good fitting looks like

  • Level & plumb — every fitting set to the room, not to the eye. Vanities, screens, WCs and rails all benchmarked.
  • Sealed properly — silicone work without smudge, gaps or bridging the wrong joint. Coloured grout matched to spec.
  • Hidden services — pipework and connections boxed in or routed so nothing visible after the fit-out spoils the line.
  • Spec respected — fittings installed exactly to manufacturer instructions so warranties are not voided.
  • Snag list closed — every small issue raised during the final walk-through is resolved before sign-off.

Fitting bathrooms supplied by the client or the showroom

We typically install bathrooms supplied by the client, the showroom or the interior designer. That keeps the specification under the client's control — and it means we can give technical feedback on items before they're ordered (drainage compatibility of a particular tray, fixing centres of a vanity, tile suitability for a wetroom floor).

FAQ · Bathroom fitters London

Bathroom fitting questions, answered.

What does a bathroom fitter actually do?+

A bathroom fitter installs the sanitaryware, vanity units, screens, taps, accessories and final fixings that make the bathroom usable. In our case the same team also handles strip-out, plumbing, electrics, waterproofing and tiling — so the whole bathroom is installed by one team end to end.

Do you supply the bathroom or only fit it?+

We typically fit bathrooms supplied by the client, the showroom or the designer — that keeps the specification under the client's control. We're happy to advise on technical suitability of products before they're ordered.

Are you insured?+

Yes — Build Masters Group Ltd holds an active certificate of insurance with public liability cover up to £5,000,000 for plumbing, heating and ventilation-related work.

How long does a bathroom fit-out take?+

Fitting alone usually takes 3–5 working days at the end of the wider installation, depending on the complexity of the spec. A full renovation including fitting is typically 7–12 working days.

Do you cover Islington and North London?+

Yes. We work across London including Islington, Angel, Hackney, Shoreditch, Camden, Clerkenwell and the wider North and Central London areas.
